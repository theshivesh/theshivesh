Custom Domain

The Custom Domain feature on Stck.me is a game-changer for creators. It lets you swap out the default Stck.me URL for your own personalized domain name. This means you can strengthen your brand identity and present a more professional image to your audience. It's all about giving you more control over how you appear online.

Problem Statement

Creators on Stck.me want more control over their online brand and a professional look. Creators with limited technical skills struggle to use their own domains and updating a website can be a hassle, making it hard to create a seamless online presence. Therefore, it's clear that there's a need for an easy way for creators to integrate and manage custom domain names on Stck.me.

  • Professionalism and Credibility: Creators struggle to appear professional and credible with standard Stck.me URLs.

  • Technical Challenges: Creators with little technical expertise find it difficult to set up and manage custom domains.

  • Ease of access: As most of the creators have very technical knowledge so we have to create simple interfaces rather than overcomplicating with visuals and illustrations. Also, we should prepare some sort of documentation/tutorial for users to follow which adding their domain name.

Goals

  • Simplify Domain Management: Develop an intuitive, user-friendly interface that guides creators through the process of integrating and managing their custom domains with minimal technical knowledge. Offer comprehensive support documentation to assist creators with domain setup and troubleshooting.

  • Increase Platform Adoption and Satisfaction: Enhance the overall user experience on Stck.me, encouraging more creators to adopt and remain on the platform. Gather feedback from users to continuously improve the custom domain feature and address any emerging needs or challenges.

  • Support Flexibility and Scalability: Design the custom domain feature to be flexible, allowing creators to update or change their domain settings as their brand evolves. Ensure that the feature can scale to accommodate a growing number of users and increased demand for custom domains.

  • Enhance Brand Identity & Improve Professionalism: Enable creators to replace default Stck.me URLs with personalized domain names that reflect their unique brand identity.

Target audience

Users who are looking for a way to enhance their written work, stories, etc. using visually captivating images.

  • Authors and Novelists: Writers who work on long-form fiction or non-fiction projects.

  • Comic Writers: Creators specializing in comic book and graphic novel writing, leveraging Picto AI to enhance and visualize their narratives through dynamic and captivating visuals.

  • Storytellers and Scriptwriters: Individuals engaged in crafting narratives for various mediums, such as film, television, or digital media, interested in visualizing aspects of their storytelling.

  • Bloggers and Content Writers: Individuals who regularly produce written content for blogs, websites, or online platforms.

  • Online Platform Contributors: Writers contributing to online platforms, forums, and communities.

Research methods

To gather valuable insights various research methods were employed. Some research methods:

  • Technical Feasibility Studies: Evaluate the technical requirements and challenges of integrating custom domains. Domain registrar compatibility, DNS settings, SSL certification, and security measures.

  • Interviews with Creators: Gather quantitative data on user preferences, technical skills, and demand for custom domains. Obtain qualitative insights and deeper understanding of user needs, challenges, and expectations.

  • Competitor Analysis: Identify best practices and features offered by competitors in the market.

  • User Journey Mapping: Visualize and understand the steps users take from discovering the custom domain feature to successfully setting it up and using it.

  • Analytics and Usage Data: Continuously monitor the performance and adoption of the custom domain feature post-launch.

  • Usability Testing: Assess the user-friendliness and efficiency of the custom domain setup process.

Wireframes & User journey

The primary goal of wireframing is to establish a clear blueprint for how users will interact with the custom domain integration process, ensuring an intuitive and seamless user experience. We included notes and questions along the way to encourage collaboration and address any user concerns.

Visual Designs

We worked in a very fast paced manner and together with PM and engineering team iterated quickly with a hi-fidelity prototype version. Along with visual I was responsible for writing down the HTML, SCSS and some JS.

Entry Point

Entry point for adding the custom domain is set under the setting section and inside the hamburger menu Under the pro features.

Setup Flow

Once the user clicks on Get started they are redirected to a setup screen for custom domain setup. where they are asked to choose the domain provider, Their domain name, and add the CNAME alias on their DNS Provider. These details are designed in such a way whereby users can easily copy and paste the values easily.

Before rolling out ....

We did Pilot Testing in the beta version with a small group of users. In this stage we checked user feedback on functionality, ease of use, and any encountered issues.

During this research we found out that most of the users were skipping the step 3 where they have to add the CNAME alias. So we decided to make a small tweak into the design and tried giving some importance to the step 3 by putting it into the next stage. Also we added a checkbox to see if the users have updated the CNAME in their DNS. We also tried to play with the UX copy making it super clear to users what needs to be done in a certain stage.

Updated designs below:

By doing this change we observed that the failure rates were significantly going down and during our re-session with the creators we found out that they were loving the new copy and steps seperations as this gives each stage it's own value.
